FAQ

Common Questions.

Gatebreaker solves the entry-level catch-22 in cybersecurity. Instead of generic advice, it uses an incident-investigation methodology to identify exact blockers, build verifiable portfolios, and help candidates bypass automated HR filters by targeting technical hiring managers directly.

Instead of just reviewing certifications, Gatebreaker evaluates your profile across 8 dimensions — each scored 0–5 against your target role's minimum entry requirements — then generates a prioritized remediation plan showing which gaps to close first for the maximum hiring impact.

A career trap where professionals accumulate 4+ certifications (Security+, CEH, CySA+) while having zero practical labs or portfolio evidence. Gatebreaker prescribes halting exam prep entirely and building documented home labs — Wazuh SIEM, Active Directory, CTF writeups — instead.

No. Gatebreaker includes a built-in mock mode that works completely offline without an API key. For live AI-powered responses, configure any of the 12 supported LLM providers (Gemini, Claude, GPT-4, Groq, etc.) by setting the appropriate environment variable before running.

Gatebreaker integrates MITRE ATT&CK for detection mapping, OWASP Top 10 for application security, NIST SP 800-61 for incident response, the FAIR framework for risk quantification, and NIST NICE (SP 800-181) for cybersecurity role taxonomy and workforce skill mapping.
