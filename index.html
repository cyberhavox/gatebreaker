Frequently Asked Questions

Structured answers about the Gatebreaker methodology, frameworks, and architecture.

💡 What is the core mission of Gatebreaker?

Gatebreaker was created to solve the "entry-level catch-22" in cybersecurity. It bypasses conventional generic advice by using an incident-investigation style gap analysis to build verifiable portfolios, focus on practical tooling, and help candidates stand out to technical managers.

📊 How does the 8-Dimension Gap Analysis work?

Instead of just checking your certifications, Gatebreaker analyzes your profile across Knowledge, Skills, Tools, Certifications, Portfolio, Network, Mindset, and Communication. It identifies exact blockers and maps out exactly what to learn and document.

🎓 What is the "Cert Collector" loop?

It is a common career trap where a professional continues to pass entry-level security certifications (like Security+, CySA+, CEH) hoping it will land them a job, while having zero practical labs or project proof. Gatebreaker forces you to halt exams and build documented labs instead.

🛠️ Which industry frameworks are integrated?

Gatebreaker integrates standard operational frameworks including the MITRE ATT&CK Framework for detection maps, OWASP Top 10 for software vulnerabilities, NIST SP 800-61 for incident handling, and the NICE Cybersecurity Workforce Framework for role mapping.
