FAQ

Common Questions

Gatebreaker solves the "entry-level catch-22" in cybersecurity. Instead of generic advice, it runs an incident-investigation style analysis of your profile to build verifiable portfolios, identify exact blockers, and help you stand out to technical hiring managers — not HR filters.

Instead of checking certifications alone, Gatebreaker evaluates your profile across Knowledge, Skills, Tools, Certifications, Portfolio, Network, Mindset, and Communication — each scored 0–5 against your target role's minimum entry requirements — then generates a prioritized remediation plan.

A career trap where professionals accumulate 4+ certifications (Security+, CEH, CySA+) while having zero practical labs or portfolio evidence. Gatebreaker identifies this pattern and prescribes stopping exam prep to build documented home labs — Wazuh SIEM, Active Directory, CTF writeups — instead.

Gatebreaker integrates MITRE ATT&CK for detection mapping, OWASP Top 10 for application security, NIST SP 800-61 for incident response, the FAIR framework for risk quantification, and NIST NICE (SP 800-181) for cybersecurity role taxonomy and skill mapping.
