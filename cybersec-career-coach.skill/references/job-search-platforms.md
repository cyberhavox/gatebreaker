## 🌐 TIER 1 — Primary Global Platforms (Web + Mobile App)

### LinkedIn Jobs
**Best For**: All cybersecurity roles globally. The single most important platform.
**Web**: linkedin.com/jobs
**App**: LinkedIn (iOS + Android) — enable Job Alerts immediately

**Power User Strategy**:
1. Set your headline: `SOC Analyst | Security+ | Splunk | KQL` — keywords first, not job title
2. Turn on **Open to Work** (visible to recruiters only, not public)
3. Set **Job Alerts** for: `"cybersecurity analyst"`, `"SOC analyst"`, `"penetration tester"` — location: [Your City]/Remote
4. Use **Easy Apply** filter to increase application volume
5. **Boolean Search Strings** (copy-paste into LinkedIn search):
```
"penetration tester" AND ("OSCP" OR "CEH") AND ("entry level" OR "0-2 years")
```

**Application Tip**: Apply within 24 hours of posting — applications drop 40% after day 3.

---


### Indeed
**Best For**: Global coverage, strong US/UK/AU listings.
**Web**: indeed.com
**App**: Indeed (iOS + Android)

**Power User Strategy**:
1. **Advanced Search** with Boolean operators:
```
site:indeed.com "cybersecurity analyst" "entry level"
"penetration tester" ("OSCP" OR "CEH") Bangalore -internship
```
2. Set **Email Alerts** for saved searches — check daily
3. Upload resume to **Indeed Resume** — recruiters search it directly
4. **Company Research**: Click company names → see all open roles + employee count (avoid tiny companies with 50+ security openings — likely staffing agencies)
5. **Salary Filter**: Use the salary range filter to remove underpaid contract roles

---

### Glassdoor
**Best For**: Salary research, employer reviews, interview process intel, job listings.
**Web**: glassdoor.com
**App**: Glassdoor (iOS + Android)

**Use Glassdoor For**:
1. **Salary Research BEFORE applying**: Search `Cybersecurity Analyst + Company Name` → see actual salaries reported by employees
2. **Interview Process Intel**: Read what previous candidates were asked in technical interviews
3. **Company Culture**: Is the security team respected? Does leadership listen to security?
4. **CEO Approval Rating**: Correlates with how well-funded and stable the security program is
5. **Job Listings**: Lower volume than LinkedIn but higher quality postings

## 🇮🇳 INDIA-SPECIFIC PLATFORMS

**Best For**: Senior and management-level cybersecurity roles (Tier 4–6). Director, VP, CISO positions.
**Web**: iimjobs.com
**App**: iimjobs (iOS + Android)

**Best For**: Tech-specific roles in cybersecurity — Security Engineer, Cloud Security, DevSecOps.
**Web**: hirist.com
**Profile Tip**: Tag all your technical skills explicitly — Hirist uses skill-matching algorithms.

**Web**: instahyre.com

**Best For**: Entry to mid-level (Tier 1–2). Strong in IT and cybersecurity support roles.

**App**: Shine (iOS + Android)

## 🤝 HIDDEN MARKET STRATEGIES (Where 70% of Senior Jobs Are)

### Strategy 1: LinkedIn Recruiter Direct Outreach
Search for cybersecurity recruiters in your region and connect with them *before* you need a job.
```
```
Message template:
> "Hi [Name], I'm a cybersecurity professional with [X years] experience in [domains].
> I'm exploring opportunities in [target role] and noticed you specialize in security hiring.
> I'd love to connect and be on your radar for relevant roles. Here's my profile: [LinkedIn URL]"

### Strategy 2: CISO/Security Leader Outreach
Target CISOs and Security Directors at companies you want to work for. They often hire directly.
```
Search: CISO + "Company Name" OR target industry on LinkedIn
```

### Strategy 3: Community Platforms (High Signal)
- **ISC2 Chapters**: isc2.org/chapters

### Strategy 4: Conference Hiring
- **DEF CON** (Las Vegas) / **Black Hat** — Hiring villages with direct company booths
- **BSides** (various cities globally) — Beginner-friendly. Hiring managers attend regularly.

### Strategy 5: Bug Bounty → Full-Time Conversion
- Report bugs to companies' security teams. Top performers get recruited.
- Companies like Google, Microsoft, and high-growth tech companies have hired directly from their bug bounty programs.

### Strategy 6: GitHub Portfolio Visibility
Your GitHub is your resume for offensive security and engineering roles.
- Publish: custom security scripts, CTF writeups, CVE PoCs, detection rules
- Star and fork projects actively — it signals engagement to technical hiring managers
- Link your GitHub prominently in every resume and LinkedIn profile

### Strategy 7: CTF Performance = Portfolio
- **HackTheBox** (hackthebox.eu): Pro Hacker rank = strong Tier 1–2 credential
- **TryHackMe** (tryhackme.com): Top 1% badge = verifiable entry-level portfolio
- **PicoCTF** (picoctf.org): Excellent for absolute beginners
- **SANS Holiday Hack / KringleCon**: Prestigious annual CTF with high visibility
